Music : « Iron » Woodkid

History

This music was written by Woodkid "Yoann Lemoine" and produced by "Mourad Belkeddar", it was recorded in 2011 and was released on March 28 of the same year, it is the first music of the artist, which he put in his first album "The Golden Age" in 2013.

Length & Genre

This music lasts 3 minutes 22. The musicals genres of this music are Experimental, Baroque pop and Neofolk.

Music video

The video clip was published on March 29, 2011 on Youtube, it currently has over 98 million views for 867 thousand likes and 50 thousand comments. The clip has a very qualitative cast as it has many famous models like Agyness Deyn, Matvey Lykov, Willy Cartier and Ross Tanner. 

Clip Award

The quality of the clip is excellent, it was even awarded the :

  • « Best Music video » at the Namur Film Festival
  • « Best Art Direction » and "Best Cinematography" at the Antville Music Video Award
  • Grand Prix at the Protoclip International Music Video Festival

Advertising

This music has been used many times, first by Ubisoft for the trailer of their game Assassin's Creed Revelations on June 3, 2011 at the E3, it was also used during the fashion show of Dior for its men's collection fall-winter 2013 A Soldier On My Own, it was finally used by Volvic for its advertising in 2014.

Cover

This music has many covers, either by the artist himself, including "Iron 2021", released on October 5, 2021, celebrating the 10th anniversary of the music, or simply by people who appreciate it.
